> Note: AgentUniverse is currently in Beta testing. If there are discrepancies between the documentation and the product, please refer to the actual product experience.

AgentUniverse provides an online service for everyone at [https://ai.infmonkeys.com/](https://ai.infmonkeys.com/), allowing you to experience AgentUniverse' features without setting up a complete environment.

You can register using your email or phone number. Currently, new users enjoy a certain amount of free quota to experience AgentUniverse' features.
